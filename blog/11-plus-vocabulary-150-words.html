11+ Vocabulary: 150 words to know

Prepify11Plus Team • 6 min read

A rich vocabulary powers comprehension, story writing, and verbal reasoning success. Here’s how to help your learner explore 150 high-impact words without drilling.

1. Group words by theme

Split the list into mini collections: curious explorers (adventurous, intrepid, inquisitive), emotion words (elated, apprehensive, resolute), and description boosters (luminous, brittle, tranquil). Theme-based practice supports storytelling.

2. Tell tiny stories

Pick three words and weave them into a two-sentence story. For example: “The intrepid fox discovered a luminous cave and felt surprisingly serene.” Use our flashcard deck to flip random combinations.

3. Mix recall modes

  • Flip & reveal: read the word, predict the meaning, then tap to confirm.
  • Typing challenge: type the definition or a synonym to strengthen spelling.
  • Quiz mode: choose the correct meaning from four options for speedy review.

4. Celebrate progress

Bookmark favourites for quick revision. Once a word feels easy, mark it as mastered and revisit in a few days. Keep celebrations light—stickers, doodles, or a joke break do wonders.

Curiosity beats cramming. A few joyful minutes each day will keep vocabulary growing steadily.
