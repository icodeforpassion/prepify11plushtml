Prepify11Plus frequently asked questions

Clarity for parents, tutors, and learners exploring our free 11+ practice tools. Everything runs locally, so you stay in control.

What is included in Prepify11Plus practice?

Prepify11Plus blends free 11+ mock tests and vocabulary flashcards covering arithmetic, reasoning, synonyms, antonyms, cloze sentences, and more. Maths missions include 20 categories with adaptive feedback.

Is Prepify11Plus really free?

Yes. The mock test and flashcards are free to use while we gather feedback from Year 5 families. You can practice as much as you like without logging in.

How do I start a practice set?

Head to the mock test section, press “Start mock,” and work through the generated questions. Progress and feedback appear after every answer.

Can I adjust difficulty?

Each mock balances core skills and stretch questions. Restart the mock for new number ranges or switch to the flashcards for vocabulary-focused sessions.

Does the mock test store my child’s responses?

No. All activity runs in your browser and resets when you refresh. Optional analytics only load after consent and keep data anonymous.

Is Prepify11Plus accessible?

We design for WCAG 2.2 AA with semantic markup, keyboard support, colour contrast, and reduced-motion options. If something feels off, email us and we’ll iterate quickly.

Who can I contact for support?

Email hello@prepify11plus.co.uk for help or to request adjustments. We love hearing what helps your learner shine.
