Using the demo

Head to the free mock test, press “Begin practice,” and work through 10–12 mixed maths and vocabulary questions. Progress updates after every answer.

Each set balances core skills and stretch questions. Restart the mock test for new number ranges or switch to flashcards for a vocabulary-focused session.

Content coverage

Number & arithmetic, fractions, decimals & percentages, ratio & proportion, factors & multiples, algebra, sequences, measures, area & perimeter, and data handling.

Synonyms, antonyms, cloze sentences, homophones, prefixes/suffixes, spelling checks, analogies, and odd-one-out drills powered by a curated 120-word bank.

Privacy & data

No. All activity runs in your browser and resets when you refresh. Optional analytics only load after consent and anonymise IP addresses.

We’ll use encrypted, UK/EU-hosted storage, role-based access, and parent-controlled sharing. You’ll be able to export or delete data any time.

Accessibility & support

Yes. Navigation, accordions, tabs, flashcards, and modals include focus states, ARIA attributes, and logical tab order.

Email hello@prepify11plus.example for help or to request adjustments.
