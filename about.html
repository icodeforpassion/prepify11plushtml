About Prepify11Plus – our story and mission

Prepify11Plus was created by former grammar school students, experienced teachers, and edtech builders who believe every child deserves access to inspiring 11+ resources. We listened to families juggling tutoring costs, packed schedules, and the pressure of selective school applications. The result is a platform that blends expert pedagogy with joyful design to make exam preparation more inclusive, transparent, and motivating.

Our curriculum team curates question banks for maths, English, and reasoning using insights from tutors across the UK. Writers and illustrators craft stories, animations, and flashcards that bring complex ideas to life, while our engineers build adaptive technology that responds to each learner’s progress. We partner with grammar schools and community organisations to host workshops, share best practice, and support outreach programmes.

Behind the scenes, we are guided by three commitments: accessibility, wellbeing, and collaboration. Accessibility means offering free pathways alongside premium extras so every family can get started. Wellbeing shows up in our positive feedback loops, mindfulness tips, and revision planners shared on the blog. Collaboration drives partnerships with tutors, parents, and pupils who co-design new features. Together we are building a supportive space that celebrates growth as much as grades.

Our values in action

Inclusive access

We provide generous free content, scholarship discounts, and community licences so every learner can experience top-tier preparation.

Evidence-informed teaching

Our tutors draw on classroom research, ongoing exam analysis, and learner feedback to update lessons each term.

FAQs About the 11 Plus Exam

What is the 11+ exam?

The 11 Plus is a UK entrance exam for grammar schools covering maths, English and reasoning.

Who can take the 11+?

Usually students in Year 5 or 6 applying for grammar school entry.

How can Prepify11Plus help?

Prepify11Plus offers free practice papers, flashcards, and personalised drills aligned with CEM and GL formats.
