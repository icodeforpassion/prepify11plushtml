Our story

Making 11+ preparation purposeful and kind

Prepify11Plus began as printed weekend mock papers on a kitchen table. Today it is a free digital practice gym where families can build maths fluency, vocabulary strength, and calm exam confidence without stress.

Built with classrooms

We prototype with Year 5 classrooms and tutoring groups. Learner reactions shape every interaction—from the sparkle on a correct answer to the words we choose for encouragement.

Practice-first philosophy

Our focus is purposeful repetition. Adaptive question banks, seeded randomness, and insightful feedback help learners know exactly what to revise next for the 11+.

Accessible by default

Keyboard navigation, ARIA live regions, reduced-motion support, and dyslexia-friendly typography are built in. If something feels off, tell us—we iterate fast.

Resources you can extend

All data lives in plain text or JSON files so you can duplicate, adapt, and share them freely. Learner progress is stored locally, keeping privacy and safeguarding front of mind.

  • Download the 500-word vocabulary list
  • Browse the motivational quote library
  • Export flashcard bookmarks as you practise
