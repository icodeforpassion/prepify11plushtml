Making practice purposeful and joyful

Prepify11Plus started as weekend mock papers printed at a kitchen table. Today it is a free digital gym where learners train for the 11+ with instant feedback, playful animations, and respectful pacing.

Built by teachers & parents

We prototype with Year 5 classrooms and tutoring groups. Real learner reactions shape every interaction, from the sparkle on a revealed answer to the words we choose for encouragement.

Practice-first philosophy

Lessons and theory live elsewhere. Prepify focuses on purposeful reps, seeded randomness for endless variety, and clear metrics so you know exactly what to revise next.

Accessible by default

Resources you can extend

All data files are plain text or JSON. Duplicate them, add your own questions, and drop them back into the data/ folder. Learner progress stays stored locally, so no logins or trackers are required.
