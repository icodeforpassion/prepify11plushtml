Vocabulary flashcards that unlock 11+ word confidence

Prepify11Plus flashcards transform tricky vocabulary into memorable stories, pictures, and sounds. Each deck focuses on exam-ready themes such as character traits, academic language, and synonyms that appear in CEM and GL reading passages. Learners swipe through definitions, sample sentences, and quick quizzes that encourage them to say the word aloud and connect it to real-life experiences. Spaced repetition technology resurfaces words right before they are forgotten, keeping revision short and effective.

Flashcards are grouped into playlists for morning commutes, evening wind downs, or weekend power sessions. Families can assign decks that match current English comprehension texts or maths problem solving vocabulary. Each card includes audio pronunciation, word families, and short writing prompts to encourage deeper understanding. Pupils can bookmark favourites, star challenging words, and unlock badges as their mastery grows.

To strengthen retrieval, pair flashcards with printable quizzes or link them to reasoning puzzles on the reasoning page. Teachers can export curated lists to share with tutoring groups, while parents monitor progress through weekly email summaries. When you are ready to expand the library further, explore the plans and pricing page for premium decks and exam-cram playlists, and keep an eye on the blog for seasonal vocabulary challenges.

FAQs About the 11 Plus Exam

What is the 11+ exam?

The 11 Plus is a UK entrance exam for grammar schools covering maths, English and reasoning.

Who can take the 11+?

Usually students in Year 5 or 6 applying for grammar school entry.

How can Prepify11Plus help?

Prepify11Plus offers free practice papers, flashcards, and personalised drills aligned with CEM and GL formats.
