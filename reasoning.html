Verbal and non-verbal reasoning drills for the 11 Plus

Prepify11Plus reasoning workouts guide learners through the full range of logic puzzles that appear in UK grammar school entrance exams. Short daily missions introduce skills such as code breaking, sequences, analogies, and syllogisms before inviting pupils to apply them in timed challenges. Each activity is aligned with the maths and English skills already mastered so reasoning becomes the bridge that connects numerical fluency and rich vocabulary.

Interactive diagrams and drag-and-drop tiles help students visualise rotations, reflections, and pattern transformations. Where images are unavailable, step-by-step explanations describe how to sketch diagrams quickly on scrap paper during exam conditions. Learners discover strategies for scanning question stems, eliminating distractors, and managing tricky multi-mark tasks. Teachers and families receive printable checklists to track completed topics and revisit weaker areas.

As confidence grows, pupils can tackle mixed mock exams that pair verbal reasoning with non-verbal sets to mimic the pressure of the real day. Each score report includes tips for cross-training using vocabulary flashcards and memory games to strengthen working memory. FAQs About the 11 Plus Exam

What is the 11+ exam?

The 11 Plus is a UK entrance exam for grammar schools covering maths, English and reasoning.

Who can take the 11+?

Usually students in Year 5 or 6 applying for grammar school entry.

How can Prepify11Plus help?

Prepify11Plus offers free practice papers, flashcards, and personalised drills aligned with CEM and GL formats.
